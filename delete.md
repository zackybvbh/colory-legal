# Colory — Data Deletion / Удаление данных

This page explains how to delete your data in **Colory**, a color-by-numbers coloring game
published by **ZackysStudio**.

Эта страница объясняет, как удалить свои данные в приложении **Colory** (раскраска по номерам)
от **ZackysStudio**.

---

## How to delete your data / Как удалить данные

### Option 1 — In the app (recommended)

1. Open **Colory**.
2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon in the top bar).
3. Scroll to the bottom, to the **Reset** section.
4. Tap **Clear all progress** and confirm.

This erases the coloring progress of all pictures on your device. If you are signed in to
Google Play Games with sync enabled, it also clears your progress stored in the cloud.

**Русский:** Откройте **Colory** → **Настройки** (шестерёнка вверху) → внизу раздел
**«Сброс»** → **«Очистить весь прогресс»** → подтвердите. Это стирает прогресс на устройстве,
а при включённой синхронизации — и в облаке.

### Option 2 — By email

Send a request to **zackysstudio@gmail.com** with the subject "Data deletion" from the email
address you use, and we will delete any data we control that is associated with your request.

**Русский:** Напишите на **zackysstudio@gmail.com** с темой «Data deletion» — мы удалим данные,
которыми управляем.

### Option 3 — Cloud data via your Google account

Coloring progress synced through Google Play Saved Games is stored in your own Google Drive
(app data folder). You can remove it from your **Google account** settings (Play Games / Drive
app data).

---

## What is deleted and what is kept / Что удаляется и что остаётся

**Deleted:**

- Coloring progress of all pictures (on the device, and in the cloud if sync is enabled).
- Preview thumbnails and the local index of started pictures.

**Kept:**

- The pictures themselves (library images and any you created from your own photos) — only the
  coloring is reset, not the pictures.
- App settings (theme, language, etc.), until you clear the app's data or uninstall the App.
- Aggregated, non-identifying analytics may be retained by our service providers (Google
  Firebase, Google AdMob) according to their own policies.

## Retention / Сроки хранения

- **On-device data** is kept until you clear it, clear the app's data, or uninstall the App.
- **Cloud-synced progress** is kept in your Google Drive app-data folder until you clear it or
  remove it via your Google account.
- **Provider data** (analytics, advertising) is retained per each provider's policy, described
  in our [Privacy Policy](privacy).
